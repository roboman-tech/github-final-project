# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy and understanding towards othersâ€™ experiences and needs

## Enforcement

Community members who are found to be in violation of this code of conduct may be sanctioned or expelled from the community at the discretion of the project maintainers.

## Responsibilities

- Participate in the community with respect for others.
- Engage in positive and productive dialogue.
- Help create a welcoming, inclusive environment.
- Report violations to the maintainers in a timely manner.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and it also applies when an individual is representing the community in public spaces.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Deliberate misgendering or use of deadnames
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission

## Enforcement Guidelines

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at [email@example.com]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
